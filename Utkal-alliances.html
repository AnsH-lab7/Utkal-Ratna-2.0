Our Story

Utkal Alliances was founded with a vision to revolutionize agriculture in Eastern India through sustainable practices. What began as a small mushroom cultivation project has now grown into a comprehensive organic farming enterprise, committed to producing premium quality products while nurturing the environment.

Our journey started in the fertile lands of Odisha, where we recognized the potential for sustainable farming methods that could yield high-quality produce while preserving the natural ecosystem. Today, we've expanded our operations to include various organic vegetables, mushrooms, and export-quality products that reach markets both locally and globally.
